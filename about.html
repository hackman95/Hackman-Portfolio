The Transition

Arabic Linguist | Civil Affairs Specialist | Software Engineer

My journey began in military service, where I served as a Civil Affairs Specialist before specializing as an Arabic Linguist. I graduated #1 in my class, mastering 4 distinct dialects—a feat that required intense discipline, analytical depth, and a relentless focus on precision.

Today, I apply that same discipline to Software Engineering. Translating complex human language dialects taught me how to decompose intricate systems into logical components, a skill that translates perfectly into writing clean, maintainable code.

I am currently completing the Vets Who Code prework program, preparing for a career in full-stack development where technical excellence meets strategic mission goals.
